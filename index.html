About Us

Building Futures Since 1997

Located at the foot of Satpura ranges, IMRD provides quality education to students aspiring for excellence in IT and Management.

Our Legacy

Institute of Management Research and Development, Shirpur is one of the premiere institutes run by the R.C. Patel Educational Trust, Shirpur. Established in 1997, IMRD was created with a vision to provide quality education to students of rural and tribal areas.

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education to students aspiring to develop a global perspective in information technology and management education. We focus on nurturing tomorrow's IT and Management professionals through comprehensive academic programs and industry exposure.

Why Choose IMRD?

With 27+ years of excellence, experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, strong industry connections, and a proven placement track record, IMRD stands as a beacon of quality education in North Maharashtra.
